Skills for Assertiveness – Disagreeing Gracefully

Disagreeing
Gracefully

Occasionally there will be disagreements.

Following are the simple step by step process will help you put your case without getting emotional , losing your integrity or losing your respect for the other person.

The Process –

  1. Affirmative statements
  2. Positive statements
  3. Indicate Process
  4. State Reasons
  5. Disagree
  6. Offer a compromise

The compromise is optional and it is for you to decide whether or not to make a concession.

Examples:

  1. “If I may, I would like to say something about that …”
  2. “I can understand from where you are coming from …”
