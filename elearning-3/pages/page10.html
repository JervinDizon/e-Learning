Listening Type: Informative & Appreciative

In Informative Listening , the most critical concern is to understand the message

For example, we listen to lectures to understand and learn on how well we listen. In Work, we listen to understand the new practices and procedures.

If we listen poorly, then we are not equipped with the information we need.

Appreciative listening is listening for pleasure and enjoyment, as when we listen to music, to a comedy routine or to an entertaining speech
