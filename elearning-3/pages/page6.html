Active Listening Process

Hearing

Hearing is the first step in the listening process and relates to the sensory perception of sound. The listener further processes the perceived sound. For learning to the effective, hearing needs to be done with attentiveness and concentration

Filtering

The next step involves sensing and filtering of heard sounds. The heard message is categorised as wanted and unwanted . The sense of judgement of the individual comes into play , that is , the filtering process is subjective and a person chooses to retain what makes sense to him.
