Question Types

  • Open Questions
    • Require responses that are often complex in nature
    • Use key words like “ who”, “ what”, “ when”, “where”, “why”, “how”
  • Closed Questions
    • Require simple answers like “yes” or “no”
    • Use key words like “ be” , “do” , “have”, “will”, “can”, “shall”, “must” and “might”
