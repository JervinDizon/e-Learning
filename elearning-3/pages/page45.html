Making Direct Question Polite

  • Add “excuse me” or “pardon me” to a direct question
    • Direct : Can you move the meeting to tomorrow ?
    • Polite : “Pardon me, can you move the meeting to tomorrow “?
  • Change “ can “ to “ Could “ in direct questions
    • Direct : Can you check the customer address ?
    • Polite : “Could you check the customer address for me “?
  • Adding “please” to the end of a direct question in combination with other strategies:
    • Direct : Can you direct me to the location address?
    • Polite : “Could you direct me to this location address , please “?
