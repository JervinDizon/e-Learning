Skills for Assertiveness – Broken Record

Broken Record

Repeat yourself again and again , until the customer gives in to your demands

Most customers capitulate after you repeat three times

Broken Record is useful when –

  1. Dealing with dissatisfied customer
  2. When the customer is likely to use put-downs
  3. Dealing with customers who are brighter and more fluent than you

This skill is very easy to use as you just have to repeat and reiterate yourself
