Excellent Verbal Communication with Customers

Words and phrases that damage relationship:

  • You don’t understand sir..
  • You don’t see my point…
  • Hold on a second…
  • Our policy says…
  • That’s not my responsibility
  • What you need to do is…
  • Why don’t you…
  • The word “problem”
  • The word “but”
  • The word “no”
