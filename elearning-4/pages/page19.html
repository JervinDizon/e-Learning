Compliance Customer

  • Control emotional expressions
  • Displays a preference for orderliness
  • Tends to express measured opinions
  • Seems difficult to get to know
  • Provide a thoughtful , well organized approach
  • Use specific questions that show clear direction
  • Provide detailed and comprehensive information
  • Never pressure the customer to make quick decisions. Allow him time to think well.
