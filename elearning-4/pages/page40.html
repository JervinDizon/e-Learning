Non Verbal Communication with Customers

  • Brief eye contact
  • Eyes wide open
  • Smiling
  • Nodding affirmatively
  • Expressive body gestures
  • Open body stance
  • Listening actively
  • Remaining silent as customer speaks
  • Gesturing with open hand
  • Clean and organized
  • Yawning
  • Frowning or sneering
  • Attending to matters other than the customer
  • Leaning away from customers as he/she speaks
  • Subdued or Minimal hand gestures
  • Staring blankly or coolly at customers
  • Interrupting
  • Pointing finger or object at customer
  • Disorganized, cluttered
