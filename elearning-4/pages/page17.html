Influencing Customer

  • Appears quite active
  • Takes social initiatives in most cases
  • Likes to encourage
  • Expresses emotional opinions(feelings)
  • Be enthusiastic
  • Avoid an approach that is highly formal
  • Take time to build rapport with the customer
  • Maintain good eye contact
  • Be a good listener
  • Plan actions that will support their opinions and ideas
  • Do not place much detailing into facts
