Dominance Customer

  • Appears to be quite busy
  • May give the impression of not listening
  • Displays a serious attitude
  • Voice strong Opinions
  • Developing strong personal relationship is not a high priority for this category of customer
  • Be as efficient, time disciplined, and well organized as possible
  • Provide appropriate details about the product with added benefits and advantages , in relevance to the customer business
  • Try to identify their primary objectives and then determine ways to support with these objectives
