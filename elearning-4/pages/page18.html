Steadiness Customer

  • Gives the appearance of being quiet and reserved
  • Listen attentively to other people
  • Tend to avoid the use of power
  • Make decisions in a thoughtful and deliberate manner
  • Take time to build rapport with the customer
  • Spend time learning about customers business and make suggestions accordingly
  • Provide personal assurance and support their views
  • Do not disagree with this type of customer , as he/ she dislikes interpersonal conflict
  • Give them the time to comprehend your responses/ explanations. Patience is important
