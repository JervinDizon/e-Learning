Excellent Verbal Communication with Customers

  • Plan your interaction/ conversation
  • Greet Customer warmly and sincerely
  • Be specific
  • Use “small talk”
  • Use simple languages
  • Paraphrase
  • Ask positively phrased question
  • Communicate to customers style
  • Agree with customers
  • Solicit customer feedback and participation

Words and phrases that build relationship:

  • Please
  • Thank You
  • I can or will
  • How may I help?
  • I understand how you feel
  • You’re right
  • May I
  • Would you mind…
  • I apologize for…
